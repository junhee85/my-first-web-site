The Wayback Machine is a digital time capsule created by the Internet Archive . Alexa Internet is being maintained with its content. In this archive, called a "three-dimensional index", users can view web pages that have been preserved through time. Preserved web pages are published after 6 to 12 months of storage. This is because the storage period is different. It doesn't track and log the website for every update, but it stores them every few weeks.
